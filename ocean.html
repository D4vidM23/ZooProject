Oceans

The ocean hosts incredile and diverse ecosystems that contain various animals.

Alantic Bluefin Tuna

Alantic Bluefin Tunas are large predatory fish that are built for speed and have great eyesight (WWF Bluefin Tuna, pp1). The largest of these fish can grow up to 13 feet (4 meters) in length and reach 2000 pounds (907kg).

North Alantic Right Whale

North Alantic Right Whales can grow to a length of 50 feet (15 m) and weigh up to 64 metric tonnes, 63,500kg, or 140,000 pounds. (Oceana North Alantic Right Whale, pp1). To reach these enormous sizes, they feed on pelagic crustaceans, like krill and copepods (Oceana North Alantic Right Whale, pp1).
