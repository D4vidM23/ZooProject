In this harsh habitat (at least for humans), polar bears, whales, and many other animals hold out the cold tempurates with their adaptions.

Leopard Seal

Living in the Antaractic, this predatory seal preys on fish, penguins, birds, squids, and crustations (Austrialian Antarctic, pp9). Leopard Seals have a slim body for swimming and can grow up to 11 feet or 3.5 meters (Austrialian Antarctic, pp1).

Polar Bear

On the opposite side of the equator, Polar Bears stroll around in the Artic. Covered in a white coat, they're able to camoflauge with their surrounding. When they're not hunting for seals, they tend to sleep (All About Polar Bears Seaworld, bullet 3).
