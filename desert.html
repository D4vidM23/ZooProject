Desert

Deserts are dry, hot, and cold. You probably won't be able to survive in extreme deserts without the proper equipment or technology.

Fennec Fox

These tan/white foxes with distinct ears live in the African Sahara measuring up to around 14 inches in length and weighing in at about 2-3 pounds (Smithson National Zoo, pp3). Their diet consists of bugs, rodents, lizards, birds, and eggs (Smithson National Zoo, pp6).

Desert Tortoise

As can be seen in the photo, a desert tortoise has a bumpy shell and strong nails used to dig dens. A desert tortoise's diet consists of cacti, grasses, leaves, and flowers (San Diego Zoo Desert Tortoise, pp9)
