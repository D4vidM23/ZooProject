Rainforest

In the deep jungle, birds can be heard chirping and the sun breaks through thick vegatation. A huge variety of animals thrive in this humid habitat.

Jaguar

Jaguars have a carnivorous diet and prey on wild hogs, deer, fish, and more (WWF Jauguars, fact 7). Additionally, they can weigh 120kg and reach more than 5 feet in length (WWF Jauguars, fact 3).
